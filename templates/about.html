ABOUT AGRINUTRIENTNET

Our Plant Nutrient Deficiency Detection System is an AI-powered web application designed to help farmers, gardeners, and researchers identify plant deficiencies quickly and accurately.

WHO WE ARE?

To empower farmers with fast, affordable and accurate nutrient deficiency detection.

Technology

Future Scope
